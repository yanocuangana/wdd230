Visual Hierarchy

Amazon

amazon.com

Visual hierarchy allows visitors to figure out the most important elements of a web page without having to read through the page. Amazon uses this technique in their website. The "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now" buttons are the main call to actions of a product page, hence the emphases in color, contrast and size of the buttons compared to the other elements on the page

Contrast

On The Grid

onthegrid.city

A basic principle of contrast is that the higher the contrast between two items, the easier it is to visually separate them. Hipster neighbourhood guide On The Grid achieves that beautifully by employing a strong blue and white design that uses brightly coloured tiles once you drill down into a category.

White Space

Burnkit

burnkit.com

Empty space is not always wasted space. In fact, when it comes to web design, it's a best practice to give your content a little breathing room. Burnkit's homepage features lots of whitespace.Each article is given generous padding, and the user can keep scrolling to reveal new material.
